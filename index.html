A cosy alpine retreat

Tucked into the quiet hillside village of Thierachern, Chalet Alpenblick blends classic Swiss warmth with modern comfort. Sink into the sofa by the wood-burning stove, cook with friends in the fully-equipped kitchen, and wake up to mountain light pouring across the valley.

The apartment sleeps up to six guests across three bedrooms, with a spacious living area, a south-facing balcony and free parking right at the door.

  • See the Spaces

Above Lake Thun, between mountain & lake

Thierachern sits on a sunny plateau just minutes from Thun’s medieval old town and the turquoise shores of Lake Thun (Thunersee). From here the whole Bernese Oberland opens up — Interlaken, Spiez, the Stockhorn cable car and the legendary peaks of the Jungfrau region are all within easy reach.

It’s the best of both worlds: peaceful countryside on your doorstep, and a lively lakeside town a short drive or bus ride away.

  • Things to Do

Adventure in every season

Hike or paraglide from the Stockhorn in summer, ski its slopes in winter, or cruise Lake Thun by paddle-steamer. Cycle the valley trails, swim in alpine waters, explore Thun Castle, or simply soak up the view with a glass of local wine on the balcony.

Whatever the season, the chalet is the perfect base camp for your Swiss adventure.
